About us

Chaifellows

Chaifellows is a franchise business that sells 24 types of tea and all items related to tea. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience, from the moment they walk through our doors. Our mission is to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. We want our customers to feel like they're part of our family, and we strive to provide them with the highest level of customer service. Our vision is to be the premier tea shop in the community. We want to be known for our delicious tea, our friendly service, and our commitment to giving back to the community. Our values are at the heart of everything we do. We believe in treating everyone with respect, regardless of their background or beliefs. We also believe in giving back to the community that supports us. We're proud to be a part of this community, and we're committed to making it a better place. We hope you'll join us on our journey.
